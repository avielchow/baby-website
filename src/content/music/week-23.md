Every cadence so far kept its word. This week, the beautiful lie. **The deceptive
cadence** — a V chord that resolves to vi, keeping every melodic promise while the bass
walks past the front door — is how composers buy one more verse, one more approach, one
more measure of ache. It's the turn the rainbow song floats on: somewhere, not here,
not yet. Eight bars, two approaches to home, one landing. The art this week isn't
arrival; it's making the room want it twice.
