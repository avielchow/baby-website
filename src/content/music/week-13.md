Before the capstone, a pilgrimage. **Pachelbel's Canon** — eight chords on a ground
bass, the wheel that hundreds of pop songs are secretly built on, and, not incidentally,
the piece more human beings have fallen asleep to than any other — turns out to be the
neighborhood you've lived in for thirteen weeks. Week 1's descending line was its front
steps. The axis was its abbreviation. This week you walk the whole wheel, play variations
over it the way Pachelbel intended, and hold one note through eight changing meanings.
The oldest lullaby, handed to the newest listener in the house.
