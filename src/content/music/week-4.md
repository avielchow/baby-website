Three weeks inside C major's own furniture; this week you borrow from the neighbors.
**The minor iv** is a single semitone of theft — A becomes A♭ — and it is pop music's
most reliable heartbreak: the moment in Creep, the sigh in a hundred Beatles bars, the
'and then it was over' of every film cue. The technique is tiny. The effect is not.
One cloud crosses the sun, the sun comes back, and the room feels different for having
watched it happen.
