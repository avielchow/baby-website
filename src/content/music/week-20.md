Some chords argue you home; this one carries you. **The eleventh chord** — a whole IV
triad standing on the dominant's shoulders, no 3rd, no tritone, no argument — is how
gospel says 'amen' and how Stevie Wonder says 'isn't she lovely,' which he wrote, not
incidentally, about a brand-new daughter. Wrapped here in the great I–vi–ii–V turnaround
with a backbeat-leaning left hand, it's the warmest dominant you'll ever resolve. Your
right hand already knows every note; the sermon is entirely in the bass.
