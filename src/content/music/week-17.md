Every borrowed color so far leaned darker or looser. This one opens the ceiling. **Lydian**
— the major scale with its 4th raised into a wonder note — is the sound of awe without
urgency: the Ghibli sky, the Force theme, light on water. Its whole grammar is one
sharpened tone and the refusal to resolve it, and its home voicing (a D chord floating on
a C bass) takes thirty seconds to learn and a lifetime to stop using. Played at 63 with
the pedal down over a sleeping boy, it is, frankly, unfair.
