This site is for us, and for the small circle of family and friends who care about the day-to-day of our little one arriving.

Two things live here. A **week-by-week guide** — the practical, sometimes overwhelming set of things it turns out you need to know at each stage. And a **journal**, which I'll be dictating into as things happen. The plan is that I speak or type loosely, and the writing gets polished into something more readable before it lands here.
