## Practical tools & helplines

- CDC **Milestone Tracker** app — official checklists by age, works offline.
- La Leche League helpline: **1-800-525-3243** + local in-person groups.
- Postpartum Support International helpline: **1-800-944-4773** — for you AND your partner.
- PurpleCrying.info "PURPLE" acronym: crying peaks weeks 2–8 and is normal.

## Structure ideas carried into the site spec

- Topic filters per week: Sleep / Feeding / Development / Safety / Mental Health.
- Trust tiers surfaced on citations: Tier 1 (federal/AAP/CPS), Tier 2 (medical nonprofit), Tier 3 (expert-reviewed editorial).
- "Week 1 Starter Pack": 3 essential websites + 2 channels to bookmark first.
- Map resources to phases (e.g. Weeks 1–2 → HealthyChildren + New Parents Academy).
- Every week: development, feeding, sleep + safe sleep, health checks/vaccines, normal vs. call-the-doctor, parent mental-health tip, one recommended video.
- Standing disclaimer: content is a summary; always consult your pediatrician.
